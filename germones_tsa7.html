Episode 1: "Dog & Chainsaw"

Denji is a young boy who lives as a devil hunter. In order to repay the debts left by his parents, he lives a poor life, taking on jobs from the Yakuza with Pochita, the "Chainsaw Devil". One day, Denji is summoned by the Yakuza, betrayed, and killed. As his consciousness fades, someone speaks to Denji.
